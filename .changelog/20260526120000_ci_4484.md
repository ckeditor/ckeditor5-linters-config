Added a new `ckeditor5-rules/css-indent` rule that enforces tab indentation in CSS files: one tab per nested block, plus one extra tab for lines inside a multi-line `( ... )`. Continuation lines outside parens and content inside parser-opaque blobs (`@starting-style`, custom-property values) are not checked.
