---
type: Major breaking change
scope:
  - eslint-config-ckeditor5
---

The CSS preset now enables a set of `@eslint/css` rules: `no-duplicate-imports`, `no-duplicate-keyframe-selectors`, `no-empty-blocks`, `no-invalid-at-rule-placement`, `no-invalid-at-rules`, `no-invalid-named-grid-areas`, `no-unmatchable-selectors`, `selector-complexity`, `prefer-logical-properties`, and `use-baseline` (with `{ available: 'widely' }`). It also enables the new `ckeditor5-rules/css-indent` and `ckeditor5-rules/no-disallowed-color-formats` rules.
